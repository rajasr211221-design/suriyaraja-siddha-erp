# 1. Introduction

The Landing Page is the public-facing website of Suriyaraja Siddha Health Care. It serves as the first point of interaction for patients, visitors, and healthcare professionals. The landing page showcases the clinic's services, doctors, facilities, and provides easy access to appointment booking, patient login, and staff login.

The landing page is designed to build trust, improve accessibility, and seamlessly connect patients with the clinic's ERP system.

---

# 2. Objectives

The objectives of the Landing Page are:

- Introduce Suriyaraja Siddha Health Care to visitors.
- Showcase Siddha healthcare services.
- Present doctor profiles and qualifications.
- Enable online appointment booking.
- Provide access to Patient Portal and Staff ERP.
- Share health education and awareness.
- Display clinic information and contact details.
- Build a professional online presence.

# 6. Landing Page Structure

## 6.1 Overview

The Landing Page is the official public website of Suriyaraja Siddha Health Care. It serves as the primary digital identity of the clinic and provides information to patients, visitors, healthcare professionals, and the general public.

The landing page is fully integrated with the Suriyaraja Siddha Health Care ERP and is designed to provide a seamless transition from the public website to the internal ERP system.

All content displayed on the landing page shall be dynamically managed through the ERP. No source code modification should be required for routine updates.

Only users with the **Administrator** role are permitted to edit landing page content.

# 7.16 Homepage Editor

## Overview

The Landing Page shall include a dedicated **Homepage Editor** that allows authorized Administrators to manage all public website content without modifying the source code.

The Homepage Editor shall function as a built-in Content Management System (CMS) within the Suriyaraja Siddha Health Care ERP.

All updates made through the Homepage Editor shall be stored in the database and reflected on the official website after publication.

The Homepage Editor shall be accessible only through the ERP.
