# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Overview

The Foundation Module establishes the design, user interface (UI), user experience (UX), branding, layout, accessibility, responsiveness, and reusable component standards for the Suriyaraja Siddha Health Care Digital Platform.

This document serves as the central design guideline for all applications developed under the Suriyaraja Siddha Health Care ecosystem, including the Official Website, Clinic ERP, Patient Portal, and future modules such as the AI Digital Assistant, Manufacturing ERP, and Multi-Branch Management System.

The Foundation Module ensures consistency, maintainability, scalability, and a professional healthcare experience across the entire platform.
